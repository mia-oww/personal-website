RetroSeek

RetroSeek is a Y2K-inspired search engine built with React and TypeScript. Created for PeachHacks Hackathon.

Built for PeachHacks with alongside four incredible upperclassmen, I personally took on the challenge of integrating the Wayback API, out of curiosity about APIs. RetroSeek differentiates between search terms and URLs, delivering either a cached vintage Google search result or archived web pages. For fun, an "I'm feeling lucky" button was integrated as well. Additionally, I focused on optimizing search speed for a smoother, nostalgic browsing experience. It was a fantastic learning experience!
